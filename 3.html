The Great Gatsby

By F. Scott Fitzgerald

reviewer Name - Rakshit

4.5/5 stars

This is a classic novel that explores the American Dream and the excesses of the Roaring Twenties. The writing is beautiful and the characters are well-developed.

To Kill a Mockingbird

By Harper Lee

reviewer Name - Rakshit

5/5 stars

This Pulitzer Prize-winning novel is a powerful exploration of racial injustice and the loss of innocence. The characters are memorable and the story is both heartbreaking and uplifting.

The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy

By Douglas Adams

reviewer Name - Rakshit

4/5 stars

This humorous science fiction novel follows the misadventures of an unwitting human and his alien friend as they travel through space. The writing is witty and the characters are quirky.
